Student Registration

Register with Bacon

  1. Fill out the registration questionnaire (link changes each season, will be updated when 2020-21 registration opens).
  2. Both the student and parent/guardian should read the handbook and the bylaws, then sign the acknowledgement form and turn it in at build.
  3. Request an invite to Slack (our primary method of team communication) by talking to any member of team leadership or by emailing baconfrclead@gmail.com.
  4. Sign up for an account on the forum. Student must have an account, and at least one parent/guardian is encouraged to have an account as well.
  5. Pay team dues of $175 per youth member to our treasurer or any member of team leadership. Deposit of $50 due upon registration, remaining balance due by September 30th.

Register with FIRST

  1. Visit firstinspires.org
  2. Click "register" in the top right and create an account
  3. Apply to team 1902

Register with 4-H

  1. Visit florida.4Honline.com
  2. Set up a profile
  3. Add a student member
  4. Apply to club EB FRC
  5. Visit the membership fees site
  6. Pay the $20 fee and send your receipt to treasurerbacon@gmail.com
